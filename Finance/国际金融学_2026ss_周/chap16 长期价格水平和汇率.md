
## 一价定律 law of one price
- assumption
	- in a competitive market
	- no transaction costs and no trade barriers
	- identical goods sold in different countries must sell foe the same price when their prices are expressed in term of the same currency
- $P_{A}^i=E_{A/B}\times P_{B}^i$ 

## 购买力平价 purchasing power parity
- 购买力平价 purchasing power parity, PPP：两国货币汇率等于两国价格水平之比
- $P_{A}=E_{A/B}\times P_{B}$  
- $P_{A}$+ >> demand for A's goods- >> demand for A's money- >> $E_{A/B}$-
### PPP和LOOP之间的区别
- loop $\Rightarrow$ PPP; PPP $\not\Rightarrow$ loop
- 绝对购买力平价：$P_{A}^i=E_{A/B}\times P_{B}^i$ 
	- makes no sense unless baskets are identical
- 相对购买力平价：$(E_{A/B,t}-E_{A/B,t-1})/E_{A/B,t-1}=\pi_{A,t}-\pi_{B,t}$ 
	- makes logic sense even if we compares different baskets
	- 相对购买力平价的推导基础：$\frac{E_{A /B,t}}{E_{A /B,t-1}}= \frac{P_{A,t} /P_{B,t}}{P_{A,t-1} /P_{B,t-1}}$ 



## 建立在购买力平价之上的长期汇率模型

### 货币分析的基本方程
购买力平价：$E_{A/B}=P_{A}/P_{B}$ 
价格水平决定：$P_{A}=M_{A} /L(R_{A},Y_{A}),P_{B}=M_{B} /L(R_{B},Y_{B})$ 

### 持续通货膨胀、利率平价和购买力平价
More often, the monetary authorities choose a growth rate of money supply.
Money growth results in an inflation at the same rate, but does not affect the long-run employment and output.
相对购买力平价：$(E_{A/B,t}-E_{A/B,t-1})/E_{A/B,t-1}=\pi_{A,t}-\pi_{B,t}$ 
利率平价：$(E_{A/B,t}-E_{A/B,t-1})/E_{A/B,t-1}=R_{A,t}-R_{B,t}$
利率与通胀 Fisher Effect：$R_{A,t}-R_{B,t}=\pi_{A,t}-\pi_{B,t}$

### 费雪效应
费雪效应 **Fisher effect**：其他条件不变，若一国的预期通货膨胀率上升，最终会导致该国货币存款利率的同比例上升。同样，预期通货膨胀率下降最终会导致利率水平下降。

$\pi$++ >> Relative PPP: $E^e$++ >> interest parity: $R_{A}$++

## 如何解释购买力平价出现的问题

- 如何解释对购买力平价不利的经验证据呢
	1. 贸易壁垒和非贸易品
		- Transport costs and restrictions on trade do exist
		- Service generally is nontradable
		- One price need not hold in two markets
	2. 非自由竞争：寡头决定价格
		- Monopoly, or, oligopoly, results in price discrimination
		- Pricing to Market: a firm sells the same product for different prices in different markets
	3. 不同国家消费模式和价格水平计量方面的差异
		1. Different purchase habit in different countries
		2. Different goods baskets in different countries
	4. 短期和长期购买力平价：价格调整需要时间
		1. Departures from PPP may be even greater in the short run then in the long run
- Different Prices of Nontradables：more expensive in the rich countries
- Balassa-Samuelson Theory
	- The lower labor productivity in poor countries implies lower wages and lower costs.
	- The ratios of capital over labor in rich countries are higher. The marginal productivities of labor in rich countries are higher than those in poor countries.

## 超越购买力平价：长期汇率的一般模型

### 实际汇率
- 实际汇率 real exchange rate：两国货币之间的实际汇率是一国商品和服务价格相对于另一国价格的一个概括性度量标准。
- 名义汇率 nominal exchange rate：两种货币的相对价格。
- 实际汇率：$q_{A/B}=(E_{A /B}\times P_{B}) /P_{A}$

### 长期均衡中的名义汇率和实际汇率
1. 相对货币供给水平的变动：M上升>>P同比例上升>>E同比例上升>>q不变
2. 相对货币供给量增长率的变动：M增长率上升>>P同比例上升>>$\pi$同比例上升>>$E_{A /B}$同比例上升>>q不变
3. 相对产品需求的变动：相对产品需求增加>>$q_{A /B}$下降>>$E_{A /B}$下降
4. 相对产品供给的变动：相对产品供给增加>>$q_{A /B}$上升，$P_{A}$下降>>E的方向无法确定
- Monetary disturbances will not change the real exchange rate (relative PPP holds)
- Disturbances in output will change the real exchange rate

## 国际利差和实际汇率

$(q_{A/B}^e-q_{A/B})/q_{A/B}=[(E_{A/B}^e-E_{A/B})/E_{A/B}]-(\pi_{A}^e-\pi_{B}^e)$ 
利率平价：$R_{A}-R_{B}=(E_{A /B}^e-E_{A/B})/E_{A/B}$ 
利差=预期实际贬值+预期通胀率差
## 实际利率平价

- 名义利率 nominal interest rate 
- 实际利率 real interest rate $r^e=R-\pi^e$ 
	- $r_{A}^e-r_{B}^e=(R_{A}-\pi_{A}^e)-(R_{B}-\pi_{B}^e)$ 
- real interest parity: $r_{A}^e-r_{B}^e=(q_{A/B}^e-q_{A /B})/q_{A/B}$.
