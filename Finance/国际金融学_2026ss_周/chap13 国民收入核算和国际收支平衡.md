
## 国民收入账户 national income accounts

### 国民产出和国民收入

国民生产总值：GNP gross national product：the value of all final goods and services produced by a nation's factors of production in a given time period

gross domestic product GDP: the final value of all goods and services that are produced *within a country* in a given time period

GDP = GNP -/+ payments from/to foreign countries for factors of production

### 资本折旧和国际转移支付

national income accounts NI
NI = GNP - Depreciation + Net Unilateral Transfers

## 开放经济中的国民收入核算

$Y=C^d+I^d+G^d+EX=C+I+G+CA$ 
$Y=C+I+G+EX-IM$ 

### 经常账户和外债

$CA=EX-IM$
净国际投资头寸$IIP$：一国对外资产与负债的差额
$Y>C+I+G,CA>0$：国内产出大于国内消费，出口大于进口，经常账户盈余
$Y<C+I+G,CA<0$：国内产出小于国内消费，出口小于进口，经常账户赤字
### 储蓄和经常账户

国民储蓄：$S=Y-C-G$
封闭经济：$S=I$
开放经济：$S=I+CA\implies CA=S-I$ 
经常账户长期盈余就会导致外汇储备大量提升
current account deficit implies a funds inflow by borrowing or negative net foreign investment.

### 私人储蓄和政府储蓄

私人储蓄：$S^p=Y-T-C$
政府储蓄：$S^g=T-G$ 

## 国际收支账户

1. 经常账户 current account：商品和服务的进出口
2. 金融账户 financial account：金融资产的国际买卖状况
	1. official reserve asset
		1. the change of foreign assets held by central banks to cushion against financial instability
	2. statistical discrepancy
		1. Data from a transaction may come from different sources that differ in coverage, accuracy, and timing
	3. all other assets
3. 资本账户 capital account：其他导致财富在国家间进行转移 like debt forgiveness, copyrigts and trademarks
### 国际收支恒等式的基本平衡

经常账户余额+资本账户余额=金融账户余额

经常账户 资本账户 金融账户
