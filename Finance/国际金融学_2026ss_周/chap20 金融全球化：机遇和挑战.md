## international risk-sharing
### small economy
$\max U(C_{1})+\beta[\pi_{1}U(C_{2}^1)+\pi_{2}U(C_{2}^2)]$
subject to $\frac{p_{1}B_{1}}{1+r}+\frac{p_{2}B_{2}}{1+r}=Y_{2}-C_{2},C_{2}^s=Y_{2}^s+B^s,s=1,2$ 
FOC: $\frac{\pi_{1}U'(c_{2}^1)}{\pi_{2}U'(c_{2}^2)}= \frac{p_{1}}{p_{2}}$
Complete Risk-Sharing requires $C_{2}^1=C_{2}^2\implies \frac{\pi_{1}}{\pi_{2}}= \frac{p_{1}}{p_{2}}$ 
Price of Risk-Free Bonds: $\frac{p_{1}}{1+r}+ \frac{p_{2}}{1+r}=\frac{1}{1+r}$ 
### two country
Home: $X$; Foreign:$X^*$
goods market clearing: $C_{1}+C_{1}^*=Y_{1}+Y_{1}^*=Y_{1}^W,C_{2}^s+C_{2}^{*s}=Y_{2}^s+Y_{s}^{*s}=Y_{1}^{W,s}$ 
claim market clearing: $B^s+B^{s*}=0$ 
FOC: $\frac{p^s/\pi^s}{1+r}= \frac{\beta U'(c_{2}^s)}{U'(c_{1})}=\frac{\beta U'(C_{2}^{s*})}{U'(C_{1}^*)}$

### 外汇市场的效率

基于利率平价的检验 interest parity
利率平价条件：$R_{t}-R_{t}^*=(E_{t+1}^e-E_{t})/E_{t}$
$E_{t+1}^e$：人们正努力预测的实际远期汇率
$u_{t+1}$：预期未来贬值时所发生的预测误差
$u_{t+1}=(E_{t+1}-E_{t})/E_{t}-(E_{t+1}^e-E_{t})/E_{t}$ 
$u_{t+1}=(E_{t+1}-E_{t})/E_{t}-(R_{t}-R_{t}^*)$ 

基于风险升水的检验 risk premium
$R_{t}-R_{t}^*=(E_{t+1}^e-E_{t})/E_{t}+\rho _{t}$
People’s expectations are unobservable ⇒ mixed empirical records

基于过度波动的检验 excessive volatility
Short-run (in 1 year): random walk model is better than structure models
Long-run: structure models outperform for long-run exchange rate movement
