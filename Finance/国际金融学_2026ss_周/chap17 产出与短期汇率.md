## 开放经济环境下总需求的决定因素
- aggregate demand: the demand of a country's goods and services by the world
	- 消费需求、投资需求、政府需求和净出口需求
	- $Y=C+I+G+CA$
- 消费需求的决定因素
	- $C=C(Y^d)$ ，$Y^d=Y-T$. 
- 经常账户的决定因素
	- $CA=CA(EP^*/P,Y^d)$
	- $CA\approx EX-IM$
	- 实际汇率提高对经常账户的影响：增加
	- 可支配收入增加对经常账户的影响：减少
- 政府需求是外生的，为了方便起见，投资也是外生的

## 总需求函数

### marshall-lerner condition: theory

$CA=EX-IM=EX-q\times EX^*$, supposes $CA=0$
$\frac{ \partial CA }{ \partial q }>0\implies \frac{ \partial EX }{ \partial q }\frac{q}{EX}-\frac{ \partial EX^* }{ \partial q } \frac{q}{EX^*}>1$ 
$D=C(Y-T)+I+G+CA(EP^*/P,Y-T)$

marshall-lerner condition: fails within 6 mons hold in one year (J curve)
实际汇率提高对总需求的影响：当满足Marshall-Lerner条件时，增加
可支配收入增加对总需求的影响：增加

## 通货膨胀偏向和政策制定中的其他问题

- inflation bias: high inflation + no gain in output
	1. 名义价格粘性. 通货膨胀偏向：Sticky price may temp government to create a politically useful economic boom
	2. If workers and firms expect it in advance workers want to raise their wages and firms increase prices
	3. In this situation, government have to use expansionary policy to prevent the recession caused by the higher prices
- Difficult to estimate and understand economic data
- Politicians decide policies
- Fiscal policy is restricted by government budget
- Policies operate with lags

## 贸易流量的逐步调整和经常账户的动态分析

### J曲线
- A country's current account worsens immediately after a real depreciation and begins to improve only some months later.
	- Reason 1：most import and export orders are placed several months in advance
	- Reason 2：it takes times for real economy to adjust

### 汇率转嫁和通货膨胀

- exchange rate pass-through: The percentage by which import prices rise when the home currency depreciates by 1%

## 流动性陷阱

- Liquidity Trap: the central bank cannot reduce R any more by increasing the money supply when R falls to zero.
- How can an expansion of money supply work?
	- Key: the expected future exchange rate
	- If the central bank can credibly promise to raise the money supply permanently, AA shifts upward.
