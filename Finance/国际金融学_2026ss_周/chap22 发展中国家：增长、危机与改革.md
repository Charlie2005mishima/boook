## 发展中国家的结构特征
- Extensive direct government control of the economy
	- restrictions on international trade
	- government ownership or control of large industrial firms
- high inflation 
	- seigniorage: 政府通过硬质钞票来购买商品和服务时所获得的实际资源
- weak credit institutions
- exchange rates are managed more heavily
- natural resources or agrivultural commodities make up an important share of exports
- corrupt practices
	- common factors: government regulation >> corruption+, development-
	- corruprtion hurts economic efficiency and growth
	- poor countries lack the resources to police corruption
