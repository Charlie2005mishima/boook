- Frog‐in‐the‐pan (FIP): investors overlook small signals (|Si | < k) during the first period
- Increased retail attention → positive price pressure(short is always difficult)
- news positioning: using random front‐page news positioning on the Bloomberg terminls: 买入股票是需要注意力的且卖空限制，所以获得高度关注的股票往往会受到价格抬升；卖出股票局限于已有的持仓，不存在注意力问题
- \# of clicks on news articles before an macroeconomics and monetary policy announcement (MMPA) is therefore a measure of uncertainty associated with that event
