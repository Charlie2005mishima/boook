# 球队
## 决策
$\max_{\mathbf{x}, I_{fac}} \mathbb{E} \left[ \sum_{t=1}^{T} \frac{\pi_t}{(1+r)^t} \right]$ 
- $\pi_t = R_t - C_t$
- $Cash_{t+1} = Cash_t + \pi_t - I_{fac, t} + \Delta Debt_t$
### 收入
$R_t = R_{gate} + R_{merch} + \mathbf{R_{prize}}$
#### 门票收入
$R_{gate} = N_{games} \cdot P_{ticket}(Q_t) \cdot \text{Capacity} \cdot \text{Att}(W_t, Pop_{team, t})$
##### 比赛轮数
固定每年41场
##### 票价
$P_{ticket}(Q_t) = P_{base} \cdot (1 + \theta \ln(1 + Q_t))$
##### 基础设施
$Q_t = Q_{t-1}(1-\delta_q) + I_{fac, t}$
##### 人气
$Pop_{team, t} = \underbrace{(1 - \delta) Pop_{team, t-1}}_{\text{品牌记忆}} + \underbrace{\alpha_1 (W_t - W_{exp})}_{\text{超预期战绩}} + \underbrace{\alpha_2 \sum_{i \in New} Pop_{player, i}}_{\text{球星引流}} + \epsilon_{trend}$
###### 球员人气
$Pop_{i,t} = Pop_{i,t-1} + \sigma (WS_{i,t} \cdot W_t)$
##### 胜率
$W_t = \text{Sigmoid} \left( \frac{\sum_{i} (WS_i \cdot E_{avail, i}) + \beta_Q \cdot Q_t}{\text{LeagueAvg}} \right) + \epsilon_{game}$
###### 个人贡献
进球 防守 关键球能力 角色位置
###### 有效出勤
$E_{avail, i} = \frac{1}{N_{games}} \sum_{k=1}^{N_{games}} \eta(S_k^{(i)})$
###### 球员伤病
$\mathbf{P}^{(i)} = \begin{bmatrix}  P_{H \to H} & P_{H \to DTD} & P_{H \to IR} \\  P_{DTD \to H} & P_{DTD \to DTD} & P_{DTD \to IR} \\  P_{IR \to H}(Q_t) & P_{IR \to DTD} & P_{IR \to IR}  \end{bmatrix}$ 
$P_{IR \to H}(Q_t) = \text{BaseRate}_i \cdot (1 + \kappa \ln(1+Q_t))$ 
###### 设备加成
$f_{Q}$ 单位递减的函数 
#### 商业收入
$R_{merch} = A \cdot (Pop_{team, t})^{\gamma}, \quad \gamma > 1 \text{ (超级明星效应)}$
#### 胜场奖金
$R_{prize} = k_{win} \cdot (W_t \cdot N_{games})$
### 成本
$C_t = \sum_{i} \text{Salary}_i + C_{tax} + C_{ops}(Q_t) + \mathbf{C_{replace}}$
#### 球员薪酬
$TPV_i = \underbrace{w_1 \frac{E[WS_i]}{\text{Cost}_i}}_{\text{竞技ROI}} + \underbrace{w_2 \frac{Pop_i}{\text{Cost}_i}}_{\text{商业ROI}} - \underbrace{w_3 \cdot \text{Var}(WS_i)}_{\text{稳定性}} - \underbrace{w_4 \cdot P(H \to IR)}_{\text{重伤风险}}$
$Salary_{i}=\begin{cases}TPV_i+\beta_{NEW}\cdot \varepsilon_{i} & i=NEW \\ TPV_i+premium_{\exp}+\varepsilon_{i} & i=OLD\end{cases}$
#### 奢侈税
$C_{tax} = \begin{cases} \tau_1 \cdot \Delta S + \tau_2 \cdot \Delta S^2, & \text{if } \sum Salary_i > \text{Cap} \\ 0, & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}$
$\Delta S = \sum Salary_i - \text{Cap}$
#### 伤病
$C_{replace} = \sum_{i \in IR} \mathbb{I}(\text{Duration}_i > \tau) \cdot \text{Salary}_{min} \cdot \Delta t$
#### 设施维护
$C_{ops}(Q_t) = C_{fixed} + m \cdot Q_t$
#### 利息
$C_{interest, t} = Debt_t \cdot r_{debt}$
### 债务
$\Delta Debt_t = \text{Lev}_t \cdot Cash_{target} - \text{Debt}_{t-1}$ 
### 约束：破产
- **破产约束**: $Cash_t \ge 0, \forall t$
- **薪资硬帽**: $\sum Salary_i \le \text{HardCap}$
- **阵容人数**: $N_{min} \le \text{Count}(\text{Roster}) \le N_{max}$
- **杠杆限制**: $\frac{Debt_t}{Valuation_t} \le L_{max}$  模型相似
