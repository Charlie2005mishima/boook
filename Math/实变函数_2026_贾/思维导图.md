	- 3.3 可测函数用连续函数来逼近
		- T，$F$紧，$f_{n}\in C(F)$，$f_{n}\rightrightarrows f\;(F)\implies f\in C(F)$ 
		- T，*Egoroff定理* $f,f_{n}\in \mathcal{M}(D),m(D)<\infty,|f|,|f_{n}|<\infty\;a.e.\,x \in D$ $f_{n}\to f \,a.e.\,x\in D\implies \forall\,\varepsilon>0,\exists\,\text{闭集}F\subset D,m(D-F)<\varepsilon$且$f_{n}\rightrightarrows f\,(F)$ 
		- L，$F$是$\mathbb{R}$中的闭集，$f\in C(F)$，则$f$可开拓成$\mathbb{R}$上的连续函数$f^*$且$\sup_{x\in \mathbb{R}}|f^*(x)|=\sup_{x\in F}|f(x)|$ 
		- L，$f\in s(D)\implies \forall\,\varepsilon>0,\exists\,f^*\in C(D),\text{ s.t. }m(\{ f\not= f^*\})<\varepsilon$ 
		- T，*Lusin定理* $f\in \mathcal{M}(D),D\in \mathcal{M},|f|<\infty\;a.e.\,x \in D\implies\forall\,\varepsilon>0,\exists\,f^*\in C(D),\text{ s.t. }m(\{ f\not= f^*\})<\varepsilon$且$\sup_{x\in D}|f^*(x)|\leq\sup_{x\in D}|f(x)|$
